Curriculum vitae

Greetings! I’m Bruce, a Ph.D. student at the UCLA HCI lab advised by Professor Xiang ‘Anthony’ Chen.

I focus on HCI+AI research, exploring the future forms of LLMs and AI agents beyond a turn-based chatbot.

Examples include: agents that can proactively engage in group conversations by modeling human-like motivations (Inner Thoughts), multimodal agents that stay in sync with real-world context (Human I/O), real-time AI that dynamically generates visuals to complement speech (Visual Captions), and a document interface that lets anyone author generative videos by simply writing (Doki).

My work has been recognized and supported by an Amazon Ph.D. Fellowship, an ACM UIST Best Paper Award 🏆, two ACM CHI Best Paper Honorable Mentions 🏅. I also did research internships at Adobe Research, Google, Meta Reality Labs and Snap Research.
